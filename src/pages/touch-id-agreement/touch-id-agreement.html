Touch ID Agreement
Use Apple Touch ID or Face ID to sign on using your biometric instead of entering your username and password.

We do not recommend using Touch ID or Face ID to sign on if you share your device. When these features are enabled, any person with a saved biometric registered on your device can sign on to the WhatsNearby app, view your account information, and complete transactions.

WhatsNearby does not store your Touch ID or Face ID biometric clientInformation.appCodeName
